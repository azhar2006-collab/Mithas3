Our Story & Reach Us

Redefining quality check processes from a small village of Chennai to your nearby parlor freezer counters.

Timeline

A Family Recipe, Scaled With Care

Mithas started with one simple mission: Ice Cream is only as good as the milk inside. Read about our journey from a small farm plant to an ISO audited manufacturing plant.

1996

The Dairy Spark

Mithas begins small operations in Vellavedu Village, starting with hand-cranked wooden tubs and raw honey fusions, focusing on purity.

2008

Audited Standards

We expand and construct a modern dairy manufacturing plant, acquiring our official ISO 9001:2008 certification to guarantee hygienic processes.

2016

Expanding the Parlor

Mithas scales up to offer 18 unique product lines, including traditional stick kulfi, cups, and chocobars, becoming a wedding caterer favorite.

2026

True Milk, True Ice Cream

With thousands of happy customers across Tamil Nadu, Mithas remains dedicated to clean, fresh, and filler-free treats.
